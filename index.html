My name is Matt Justus and I aspire to be a Graphic Designer. I am 25 years old and have lived in Cary, NC my entire life. I enjoy expressing myself through design and like being creative. I was an Environmental Science major at UNC Charlotte but decided to change paths, I chose to attend Wake Tech and pursue a Graphic Design degree. My favorite thing to do in my spare time is spend time with family and friends.

PROJECTS

Project Zoo

Technology used:Indesign, Illustrator

Feeback:Praised for creativity on this project for the visual design of the grass just beneath the logo.

Process: I was given information about the zoo and it was then my job to create a poster/flyer that encapsulates the zoo's ideas, style, and goals. I enjoyed designing this project because I loved going to the North Carolina Zoo growing up.

Wellcome Realty

Technology used: Indesign, Photoshop

Feeback: Received an A+ grade for the project

Process: It was my job to put the resources given to me together and create a visually appealing page through color and page setup , to create a cohesive design for the fictional "Wellcome Realty". I enjoyed creating this design because I got to design in my own way and create something that I think is visually pleasing.
